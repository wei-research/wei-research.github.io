Wei Liu (刘伟)

Short Biography

I'm a Senior Lecturer (equivalent to Associate Professor in North American universities) and the Data Science Research Leader at the School of Computer Science in the University of Technology Sydney (UTS). I obtained my PhD degree in Data Science research at the University of Sydney (USYD). I work on machine learning and data science research, with focuses on adversarial machine learning, game theory, causal inference, and multimodal learning. I've received government and industry grants with over $1.3 million funding since 2015.
